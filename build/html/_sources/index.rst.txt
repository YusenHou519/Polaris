

Polaris
=======
Polaris is an advanced computational command line tool tailored for rapid and accurate detection of chromatin loops across a wide range of high-resolution 3D genomic contact maps. It is compatible with various data types, including bulk Hi-C, single-cell Hi-C, Micro-C, DNA SPRITE ......
